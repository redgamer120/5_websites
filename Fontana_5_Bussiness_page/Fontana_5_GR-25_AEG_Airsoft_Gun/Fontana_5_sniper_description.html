The GG GR-25 is an excellent reproduction of the now famous SR-25, which was designed as an accurate semi-automatic rifle for the U.S. Navy Seals. The GR-25 has a full metal SR-25 style receiver etched with the GG logo, model, and serial number on the magazine well. Also, the GR-25 has a one piece metal outer barrel and a free float RIS handguard; that includes a replica gas tube for added realism. In order to trump the other models on the market G&G equipped the GR-25 with a functional charging handle and bolt stop. The functional bolt stop not only allows the user to adjust his hop-up without holding back the charging handle, it creates a satisfied feeling when you tap the bolt stop and hear the bolt slam closed.
