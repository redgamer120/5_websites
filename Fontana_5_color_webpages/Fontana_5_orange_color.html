Welcome to color Psychology of Orange!

"Not my absolute favourite, but here is my insight on orange. It's very energetic, it's used to draw attention, it's flashy and pop-artish, it's enthusiastic, warm and a bit childish. It also reminds me of Autumn, cats, tulips, ginger hair, cheese, sweet potatoes, carrots... The list just goes on and on!" - Alice

  1. Orange is a combination of yellow and red and is considered an energetic color.
  2. Orange calls to mind feelings of excitement, enthusiasm, and warmth.
  3. Orange is often used to draw attention, such as in traffic signs and advertising.
  4. Orange is energetic, which is perhaps why many sports teams use orange in their uniforms, mascots, and branding.
