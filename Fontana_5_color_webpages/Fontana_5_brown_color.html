Welcome to color Psychology of Brown!

"Brown is a color that oozes security. It symbolizes HOME, contentment and peace and quietness. The browns are colors of good furniture and favorable smells thus imparting happiness. I feel confident when wearing browns." - Marti
  1. Brown can also create feelings of sadness and isolation. In large quantities, it can seem vast, stark, and empty, like an enormous desert devoid of life.
  2. Brown brings to mind feelings of warmth, comfort, and security. It is often described as natural, down-to-earth, and conventional, but brown can also be sophisticated.
  3. Brown is a natural color that evokes a sense of strength and reliability. It is often seen as solid, much like the earth.
