Welcome to color Psychology of Red!

"Red is passion. Red means you are alive and face life full throttle with pedal to the metal!" --Margaret

"My favourite colour as a child has always been red. I love red because it is vibrant, loud, and it presents to me strength." --Sandra

"Wearing red makes me feel confident." --Lotarot

  1. Red is a bright, warm color that evokes strong emotions.
  2. Red is associated with love, warmth, and comfort.
  3. Red is also considered an intense, or even angry, color that creates feelings of excitement or intensity.
  4. Consider how red is used in language: redneck, red-hot, red-handed, paint the town red, seeing red
